Pasta e Fagioli

its the famous cracker Cracker Jacks

ingredients

  • 1 cup Peanuts -- warmed
  • 1 cup Butter
  • 5 cups Popped corn -- warm
  • 2 cups Brown sugar -- packed 1/2 cup Light corn syrup 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda

How to make it:

Pop corn; keep warm in oven. Warm nuts in pan. In saucepan, melt butter; add br own suagr and syrup. Cook till thick then add baking soda; stir. Pour over warm ed popped corn and peanuts. Stir well. Let cool and munch down.
